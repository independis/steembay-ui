What is steembay?

steembay is a blockchain based auction system to sell/buy goods and services for cryptocurrency (STEEM/SBD).

Why another auction platform?

There are almost no ways to transfer cryptocurrencies directly into goods and services. The point of steembay is to create a marketplace where people can earn and use their cryptos in a way that is more than mere investment and speculation. An auction platform is a great market to figure out the real value given by the people to a currency, a service or an item.

Why the STEEM blockchain?

The STEEM Blockchain provides a censorship resistant, fast and reliable basis for auctioning. There is no way to cheat the bidding process.

The STEEM Blockchain provides an integrated and very fast transferable currency (SBD / STEEM), which is available all over the world (3 Second transfers without any fees or limitations).

The STEEM Blockchain already provides a home for an amazing and growing community of content creators, artists, writers and bloggers. More than a million created accounts speak their own language.

How to start?

All you need is a STEEM account. You can register at SIGNUP. You can use the free queue (1-2 weeks) or instant access for an amount of about 4$.

To create or bid on auctions you can use the steembay UI (beta), which is optional but gives you a possibility to see only steembay related content. Steembay is working since November 2017 on any frontend of the steem-blockchain (such as steemit.com or busy.org).

How to create your own auction? How to bid for an auction?
Take a look at the tutorial or use the steembay UI (beta).
