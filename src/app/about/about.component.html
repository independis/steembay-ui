What is steembay?

steembay is a blockchain based auction system to sell/buy goods and services for cryptocurrency (STEEM/SBD).

Why the STEEM blockchain?

We have a STEEM platform that is censorship resistant.
We have a currency (STEEM/SBD) that is available all over the world.
We have a transfer speed that is amazing (3 sec. which is faster than anything in the world).
We have an amazing community full of creativity.

How to start?

All you need is a STEEM account. You can register at SIGNUP.
